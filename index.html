Hello, I am Rishikesh Jaiswal

Remade Pistol Whip in VR using Unity.
Learnings:

  1. Heeding advice of experienced people
    It would have been astronomically harder if I hadn't taken help from people who knew what they were doing.

  2. Programming in C# 
    From knowing nothing at the start to having a basic recreation of Pistol Whip felt good. I was really happy with the result.
    ​
  3. ​Good practices (thanks MoZ!)
    Mozzy is my senior and he taught me to organize assets and scripts in Unity.

  4. Shooting mechanics in a VR environment
    Learning to code by solving little problems and searching for guidance, advice and resources online is what I found helpful. Brackeys, Valem Tutorials on YouTube and some searching on Stack Overflow helped me to come up with a working.

  5. ProBuilder modelling tool, Animating objects and ​Environment lighting in Unity​
Developer Log:

  1. Planning was improper and I had to rush to finish the game. Initial time (2 weeks!) was spent to plan the mechanics that would be implemented. 

  2. First the level was built in ProBuilder and it was initially tricky to use. 

  3. ​Week 4: Gun models were added and the player movement was set up.

  4. Week 5: Gun shooting and reload mechanics were troubleshooted and implemented. There was an issue where bullet just instantiated at the barrel and stayed there.
    ​
  5. Week 6: Testing the game.
